⚪⚫ NINE MEN'S MORRIS RULES

Classic rules on a board with three concentric squares:
• PLACING: Players take turns placing their 9 pieces on empty intersections
• MILLS: Form 3 in a row along a line to remove an opponent's piece
• MOVING: After all pieces are placed, slide pieces to adjacent empty spots
• FLYING: With only 3 pieces left, move to any empty intersection
• You cannot remove a piece in a mill unless no other pieces are available
• WIN: Reduce opponent to 2 pieces or block all their moves
